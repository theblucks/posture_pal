Our Rating Methodology

Our posture analysis and rating system is built upon established principles from scientific literature and clinical best practices. We synthesize information from various reputable sources to provide a comprehensive and accurate assessment of your posture. Below are some of the key resources that inform our ratings for each specific area of posture.

Proper head alignment is crucial for avoiding neck strain and maintaining a healthy posture. It is assessed by observing the position of the head in relation to the shoulders and the natural curve of the neck.
